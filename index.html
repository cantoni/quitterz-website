Quitterz — an app to celebrate what you're not doing.

We’ve got a public TestFlight!

Quitterz tracks the last time you did the thing you don’t want to do anymore, whatever that thing is. It gives you a medallion you can look at to remind yourself why you don’t do that thing. (You can play with the medallion — it’s a little fidget toy.) Add the medallion as a widget and watch it change over time.

You're already not doing the thing you don’t want to do. Keep not doing it.
